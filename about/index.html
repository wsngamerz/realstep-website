About

Hello there and welcome. We are realstep and it’s nice to meet you!

We are a small group of young aspiring developers who have gotten together to form what I guess you would call a ‘company’ or a ‘studio’ but we prefer to call it realstep. Ever since we first launched in 2017 we have been working both together as a team and individually on small projects such as TapZ Incremental and some bigger ones like Wills Media Server.

Now, how we started is a really simple story. I was asked by zack whether I wanted to make a game with him and of course, I said yes so we started brainstorming ideas and eventually, somewhere in that stage, we decided to start up RealStep. However, the game just went out of the window yet the studio stayed.

Usually an about us page will have a massive paragraph of ‘why we are better than our competitors’ but to be honest, we really don’t care about other competition, we just love creating products that function and whether it works for anyone else is an added bonus.

Anyway, we hope that you enjoyed reading our short introduction and enjoy yourselves looking though our site.

William Neild
RealStep Joint CEO
